Why I made this

This isn't just a project. It's a simulation of empathy. Living with a chronic condition is a constant background process — one you never signed up for, but must manage every day. I wanted to make that invisible toll visible. To show that it's not just the tasks that take energy — it's everything around them.

With thanks to Michael and Eric — your belief shaped this system more than you'll know.
